Space Advent

Status: released since 15.11.2017

In the expanses of intergalactic spaces, there is a legend about the captain of a spaceship that coped alone with a multitude of monsters during an asteroid threat. In the game Space Advent, you can test your might as a legendary captain. You will encounter monsters and asteroids that will attack you, your goal is to survive at any costs. In the arsenal of the spaceship, there are several types of weapons, as well as an electric super strike that will destroy all enemies at once. Do not forget to catch bonuses that will fix your spaceship and refill your equipment. Earn points while destroying monsters and asteroids and survive as long as possible.
