Sigma rush

Status: released since 18.03.2017

Game info

"The Sigma" object is a particle of a meteorite called the Sutter's Mill meteorite, which flew over the surface of the Earth in April 2012, scattered their red-hot debris. Very few people know that not all particles were destroyed when a meteorite fell. For 5 years, scientists have studied the celestial body, finding that it appeared in the first days of the existence of our solar system. In the course of the latest tests, the scientists of the laboratory activated the living cells contained on a meteorite surface, thereby opening "The Sigma" object, which managed to get out from the research center's walls. Your task is to help "The Sigma" object to escape as far as possible. In the space tunnel, you will find various bonuses that will help you to overcome the distance.
