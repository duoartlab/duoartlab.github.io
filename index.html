Introduction ↴

We are the "Duo art laboratory" - An independent game development

— Our game development journey started from simple curiosity and intention to bring some of our game ideas into reality. We are experimenting with illustration, animation, 3D modelling, music composing and all the stuff which we need to master in order to create better and better games. Due to this journey seems to be a long one, you can witness our progress throughout this website and contact us with any feedback you would like to share with us.

Some notes ↴

Game concepts

We create game concepts from a brainstorming process and some schema drafts before starting the actual development process. This helps us to predict some of the "unexpected" scenarios and unpredicted use cases.



Music composing

Sound effects and music composing is a difficult process. There is no predefined way that can be followed which will lead to a great sound effect or a music sample.
